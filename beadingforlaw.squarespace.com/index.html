Who we are

Beading for Law is a youth-led non-profit that provides youth K-12 with workshops and resources on constitutional rights and current political events.

Our services.

  • Host in-person and online workshops

    We conduct two online workshops each month, focusing on topics that interest the youth.

    Our in-person workshops take place in New York, hosted in local libraries, schools, or community spaces.

  • Provide volunteer opportunities

    We offer numerous volunteering opportunities, including roles in management and on the board of members. These positions enable youth to engage in meaningful change and enhance their resumes significantly

  • Educate our future generation

    Just 5% of adults are familiar with amendments 1-5.

    Many Americans lack sufficient knowledge of their rights.

    Our goal is to foster a more legally informed society, beginning with our younger generation.

Misson Statement

Beading For Law is dedicated to
empowering youth by providing legal
education, cultivating self-advocacy, and
promoting systemic change through legal
literacy across the United States.
